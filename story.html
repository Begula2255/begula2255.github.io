Our Story

I've always loved caramel apples. I wanted to sell them for my company,
as it seems that the restaurant market is a very good investment.
So, I was inspired by the name of the Macintosh computer, and obviously there is an apple called the McIntosh.
So I Photoshopped caramel onto the apple logo, and called it a logo.
But caramel apples didn't seem like enough, so I added chocolate to the apples and changed the logo to be chocolate.
